My favourite genre to read is Science Fiction, though it is considered a dead genre now (by traditional or legacy pub), which won’t buy SF any more.

For a long time, if you went to the top 100 on Amazon’s SF list, you found vampire romance novels. I stopped going there.

On the other side, the SF community is responsible for its own death. Their snobby anything not hard science is not Scifi attitude has driven most casual readers. Which leads to books with lots of science, but a boring story.

I wish we were back in the golden age of SciFi.
